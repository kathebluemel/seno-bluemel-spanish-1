### ğŸ“˜ Pedagogical Blueprint: How to Implement this Lesson

**1. The "Tener" vs "Ser" Distinction:**
Students will try to use one verb for everything. Consistently use gestures: a "hugging" motion for *Tener* (possessing years) and a "pointing to the sky/clock" motion for *Ser* (the state of time).

**2. The "De" Bridge:**
In dates, the "de" is non-negotiable (*el siete de mayo*). In time, "de la" is the bridge to the period of day (*de la tarde*). 

**3. 1:00 is Special:**
Remind students that *Es la una* is the only time that uses the singular "Es" because 1 is a singular hour.
