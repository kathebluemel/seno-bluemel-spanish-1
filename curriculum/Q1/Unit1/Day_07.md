### ğŸ“˜ Pedagogical Blueprint: How to Implement this Lesson
> *A guide for shifting from English logic to Spanish numerical identity.*

**1. Un-learning "I Am":**
Students will instinctively want to say "Soy 14." You must treat *Tener* as a "Possession of Experience." Use a physical prop (a box or bag) to represent "holding" your years.

**2. The Cognate Trap:**
Months like *Abril, Mayo, and Septiembre* are so close to English that students often mispronounce them with English vowels. Emphasize the **Spanish Vowels (A-E-I-O-U)** during the input phase.

**3. The Birthday Line-Up as Assessment:**
This is your "Chaos Management" check. If the line is accurate at the end of 12 minutes, the class has reached "Functional Fluency" for this objective.
