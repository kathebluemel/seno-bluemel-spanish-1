### ğŸ—ºï¸ Core Activity: The 21 Countries Deep-Dive (25 minutes)

**Instructional Strategy: Collaborative Mapping**
The teacher projects a blank world map. In small groups, students must work together to label all 21 Spanish-speaking countries. 

**Global Diversity Focus:**
The teacher rotates through groups, providing "Regional Clues" to help them find the harder-to-locate nations:
- *"Look for the only Spanish-speaking country in Africa (Equatorial Guinea)."*
- *"Find the largest island in the Caribbean (Cuba)."*
- *"Identify the long, skinny country on the western coast of South America (Chile)."*
- *"Find the country that connects North and South America (Panama)."*
