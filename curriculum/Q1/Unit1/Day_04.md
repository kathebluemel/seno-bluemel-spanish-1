### ğŸŒ Cultural Spotlight: The Spectrum of Formality

The 21 Spanish-speaking countries utilize register in nuanced, fascinating ways:

* **The Colombian Exception:** In many regions of **Colombia**, *usted* is used between close friends and family to signal warmth and deep respect. It is not "cold"; it is a linguistic embrace.
* **The Southern Cone (Argentina/Uruguay):** *Vos* is the standard informal "you." Using it is a marker of regional pride and authenticity.
* **The "Usted" of Respect:** Across most of the Spanish-speaking world, *usted* is used with elders, teachers, medical professionals, and strangers. It is a "social shield" of courtesy.
